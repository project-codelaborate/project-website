Quest Photography

Quest Photography is a photography and graphics design company run by our head of graphics design, Omar Ahemd. They have been a great help in creating our logo and other graphics.

Radicubs Robotics

Radicubs Robotics is a community based robotics team located in Frisco, TX. Every year they compete in a competition known as FIRST Robotics Competition. Radicubs Robotics is a direct community affiliate with Project Codelaborate since Project Codelaborate was founded by members of the Radicubs programming team.

Fostering a supportive and collaborative community around programming.
